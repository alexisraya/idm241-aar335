Walmart Microinteraction - Beta Build

Interaction: Adding an item into the cart

Notes

Triggers

  • Clicking on the “+ Add” button
  • Hovering on the “+ Add” button
  • Hovering off the “+ Add” button
  • Clicking on the plus sign
  • Clicking on the minus sign

Rules

  • The pill surrounding the add button increases its length by 50px
  • The words “+ Add” changes to “- 1 +”
  • The item is stored in a data base for the cart
  • (On Hover) pointer changes to cursor
  • (Off Hover) pointer changes to default
  • When the plus sign clicked, total shown in the button increases by one
  • When the plus sign clicked, variable total increases by one
  • When the minus sign clicked, total shown in the button decreases by one
  • When the minus sign clicked, variable total increases by one
  • When the total is at 1 and the user clicks on the minus sign again, the button goes back to the "+ Add" default and the item is removed from the data base for the cart

Feedback

  • The pill surrounding the add button increases its length by 50px
  • The words “+ Add” changes to “- 1 +”
  • (On Hover) pointer changes to cursor
  • (Off Hover) pointer changes to default
  • When the plus sign clicked, total shown in the button increases by one
  • When the minus sign clicked, total shown in the button decreases by one
  • When the total is at 1 and the user clicks on the minus sign again, the button goes back to the "+ Add" default

Loops and Modes

  • No loops
  • Cart total increases by 1, when the item is added into the cart, the button’s text changes from “+ Add” changes to “- 1 +”
  • When the plus sign or minus sign is clicked, the cart total changes by 1 respectively and stays to the new total
  • When the total is at 1 and the user clicks on the minus sign again, the button goes back to the "+ Add" default
