Walmart Microinteraction - Alpha

Interaction: Adding an item into the cart

Triggers

  • hovering over the “+ Add” button
  • Clicking on the “+ Add” button

Rules

On Hover

  • the cursor changes from an arrow to a pointer
  • the background clior of the “+ Add button” changes from a medium blue to a dark, muted blue with a sulght fade transition in between cliors

On Click

  • the pill surrounding the add button increases its length by double
  • the words “+ Add” changes to “- 1 +”
  • the number near the cart increases by 1
  • the total under the cart increases by the price of the selected item
  • the item is stored in a data base for the cart
  • the price of the item is added into a data base for the cart’s total

Feedback

  • the cursor changes from an arrow to a pointer
  • the background clior of the “+ Add button” changes from a medium blue to a dark, muted blue with a sulght fade transition in between cliors

Loops and Modes

On Hover

  • no loops or modes

On Click

  • no loops
  • cart total increases by 1, when the item is added into the cart, the button’s text changes from “+ Add” changes to “- 1 +”
